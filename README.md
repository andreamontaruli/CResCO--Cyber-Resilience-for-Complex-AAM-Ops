CResCO – Cyber Resilience for Complex AAM Operations
Supplementary Material Repository

Welcome to the official repository of CResCO (Cyber Resilience for Complex AAM Operations), a PhD research project funded by Engage 2 – SESAR 3 KTN and conducted at Sapienza University of Rome, Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering.

This repository collects supporting documents, intermediate results, figures, posters, literature datasets, and additional material produced throughout the project lifecycle.
